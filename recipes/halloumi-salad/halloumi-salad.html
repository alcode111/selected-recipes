Halloumi salad

Description

A delicious, easy-to-make, salad recipe. 20 minutes of "efforts" for one of your 5-a-day.

Ingredients

  • 2 large oranges
  • 11/2 tbsp whole grain mustard
  • 11/2 honey
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 3 tbsp olive oil, plus extra for frying
  • 2 large carrots, peeled
  • 225g block halloumi, sliced
  • 100g bag watercress or baby spinach
