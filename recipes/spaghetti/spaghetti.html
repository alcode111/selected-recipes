Spaghetti with fennel, anchovies, currants, pine nuts and capers

Description

A no-hassle family supper in under 30 minutes. A green recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 fennel bulbs
  • 400g spaghetti
  • 50ml extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, finely sliced
  • 100g jar anchovy fillets in olive oil, roughly chopped
  • 1/2 tsp dried chili flakes
  • 70g currants, soaked in boiling water for 5mins and drained
  • 2 1/2 capers, rinsed
  • 1 small pack flat-leaf parsley, leaves picked and finely chopped
  • 75g pine nuts, toasted
  • good squeeze lemon juice

Steps

Step 1

Quarter the fennel bulbs. Remove the coarse outer leaves and trim the tips (cut off and reserve any fronds). Remove and discard the core from each quarter, then chop the rest of the fennel./p>

Step 2

Cook the spaghetti in a pan of boiling, lightly salted water. Meanwhile, heat the oil in a large frying pan. SauteÃÅ the fennel until soft, then add the garlic and cook gently until pale gold. Add the anchovies, press with a wooden spoon, then add the chilli. When the anchovies fall apart, toss in the currants, capers, parsley and most of the pine nuts.

Step 3

Drain the pasta and immediately add it to the frying pan. Add the lemon juice and season to taste. Toss together, then serve straight from the pan or transfer to a large warm serving bowl. Scatter over the remaining pine nuts and any fennel fronds.
