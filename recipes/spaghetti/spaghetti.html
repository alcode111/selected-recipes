Spaghetti with fennel, anchovies, currants, pine nuts and capers

Description

A no-hassle family supper in under 30 minutes. A green recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 fennel bulbs
  • 400g spaghetti
  • 50ml extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, finely sliced
  • 100g jar anchovy fillets in olive oil, roughly chopped
  • 1/2 tsp dried chili flakes
  • 70g currants, soaked in boiling water for 5mins and drained
  • 2 1/2 capers, rinsed
  • 1 small pack flat-leaf parsley, leaves picked and finely chopped
  • 75g pine nuts, toasted
  • good squeeze lemon juice
